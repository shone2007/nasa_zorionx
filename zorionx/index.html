Build a space Biology knowledge Engine

Enable a new era of human space exploration! NASA has been performing biology experiments in space for decades, generating a tremendous amount of information that will need to be considered as humans prepare to revisit the Moon and explore Mars. Although this knowledge is publicly available, it can be difficult for potential users to find information that pertains to their specific interests. Your challenge is to build a dynamic dashboard that leverages artificial intelligence (AI), knowledge graphs, and/or other tools to summarize a set of NASA bioscience publications and enables users to explore the impacts and results of the experiments these publications describe.

  • Access all NASA bioscience research in one intelligent platform.
  • Visualize how space environments affect life through dynamic graphs.
  • Empowering scientists to prepare for Moon and Mars missions.

Why Our Website Useful

Unlocking Hidden Biological Knowledge

NASA has accumulated decades of bioscience data from Moon and Mars research, but much of it is fragmented and difficult to access. With over 608+ publications scattered across repositories, scientists struggle to find relevant, actionable insights needed for future missions.

The Solution: Transforming Data into Dynamic Knowledge

Our platform bridges this gap by turning static bioscience data into dynamic, searchable, and visual knowledge. Using AI and knowledge graphs, it simplifies complex data, making it easy to explore, summarize, and connect experiments that were once buried in archives.

The Impact: Empowering the Future of Space Exploration

By providing intuitive tools and deep insights, our system empowers: Scientists to generate new hypotheses Mission architects to plan safer, more efficient explorations Managers to identify research gaps and investment opportunities Together, this drives smarter decisions and accelerates humanity’s journey to the Moon and Mars.
